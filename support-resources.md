---

## Suggested Questions for Legal or Advocacy Help
- do you help with SSI and/or SSDI applications?
- do you assist before denial, after denial, or both?
- what documents should be gathered before intake?
- are there income guidelines for your services?
- do you provide direct representation or only referral?
- what are typical response times?
- what should be done while waiting for intake?

---

## Suggested Questions for Nonprofit Financial Counseling
- do you offer nonprofit credit counseling?
- do you help with hardship planning for immediate delinquency risks?
- do you provide debt management plans?
- what information should be prepared before the first session?
- are there any fees?
- what outcomes can realistically be expected?

## Risks
- waiting to ask for help until after a denial or crisis
- contacting resources without a clear summary of needs
- losing track of who was contacted
- assuming one organization covers all needs

---

## Mitigation
- do first outreach early
- use the outreach log for every contact
- prepare a short situation summary in advance
- keep separate notes for benefits help and debt help
